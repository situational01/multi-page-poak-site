Maternal & Child Health

Healthy mothers, thriving children, stronger communities

About the Program

POAK's Maternal & Child Health program works to reduce maternal and child mortality in underserved communities by training community health volunteers, improving nutrition, and increasing access to healthcare services.

What We Offer

  • Community Health Volunteer Training
  • Prenatal & Postnatal Care
  • Nutrition Education
  • Kitchen Gardens

Program Impact

3,000+ Mothers Reached
50+ CHVs Trained
40% Malnutrition Reduction
