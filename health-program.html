Maternal & Child Health

Healthy mothers, thriving children, stronger communities

About the Program

POAK's Maternal & Child Health program works to reduce maternal and child mortality in underserved communities by training community health volunteers, improving nutrition, and increasing access to healthcare services.

What We Offer

  • Community Health Volunteer Training - Training local women to provide basic healthcare and referrals
  • Prenatal & Postnatal Care - Health education and support for pregnant and new mothers
  • Nutrition Education - Teaching families about balanced diets and child feeding
  • Kitchen Gardens - Helping families grow nutritious vegetables at home
  • Health Facility Referrals - Connecting mothers to hospitals and clinics

Program Impact

3,000+ Mothers Reached
50+ CHVs Trained
1,200+ Kitchen Gardens
40% Malnutrition Reduction
