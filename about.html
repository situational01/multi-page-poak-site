About POAK

Fighting poverty in Kenya since 7th December 1997

Our Story

POAK (Poverty Africa - Kenya) was founded on December 7th, 1997 by a group of community leaders who saw the urgent need to address poverty in their local communities. What started as a small grassroots initiative has grown into a recognized nonprofit organization serving communities across 8 counties in Kenya.

For over 28 years, we have remained true to our founding principle: that sustainable change comes from empowering communities to lead their own development.

Founded 7th December 1997 28+ Years of Service

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To alleviate all forms of poverty in Kenya and actively involve all sectors of society in achieving this goal, improving living standards for Kenyans.

Our Vision

A Kenya free from poverty where every individual has equal opportunities to thrive, with empowered communities driving their own sustainable development.

Core Values

Equity & Inclusion

Every Kenyan deserves equal opportunities regardless of background.

Community Empowerment

We place communities at the center of development.

Integrity & Accountability

We uphold transparency in all operations.

Partnership & Collaboration

We work with government, NGOs, and communities.

Sustainability

We promote long-term solutions to poverty.

Innovation & Learning

We embrace creative approaches to program effectiveness.
