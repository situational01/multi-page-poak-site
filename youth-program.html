Youth Digital Skills

Empowering Kenyan youth with skills for the digital economy

About the Program

Our Youth Digital Skills program trains unemployed and out-of-school youth in high-demand digital skills, connecting them with internship opportunities and job placements. Since its launch, the program has transformed the lives of hundreds of young Kenyans.

What We Offer

  • 3-month intensive training
  • Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React)
  • Graphic Design (Adobe Suite, Canva)
  • Digital Marketing (Social media, SEO)
  • Internship placement
  • Job connections with 15+ partner companies

Program Impact

500+ Youth Trained
80% Job Placement
15 Partner Companies
