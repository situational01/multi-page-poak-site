Mental Health & Wellness

Breaking the silence, restoring hope

About the Program

POAK's Mental Health & Wellness program provides community-based mental health support through trained counselors and peer support groups. We focus on perinatal mental health for new mothers and youth mental health for adolescents.

What We Offer

For Mothers: Perinatal Mental Health

  • Screening for Postpartum Depression
  • Individual Counseling
  • Mother Support Groups

For Youth: Peer Support

  • Youth Peer Counseling
  • School Mental Health Talks
  • Safe Spaces for Youth

Program Impact

800+ Counseling Sessions
15 Support Groups
300+ Mothers Screened
25 Peer Counselors
